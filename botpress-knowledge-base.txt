## What is Integrative Veterinary Medicine?

Integrative veterinary medicine means using the full range of available evidence-based treatments — both conventional and complementary — to achieve the best possible outcome for the patient. Dr. Claudia holds science and evidence at the core of everything she does. She does not recommend any therapy without a solid evidence base.

What integrative medicine adds is breadth. For a cat with chronic arthritis, for example, conventional pain management (NSAIDs, gabapentin) may be effective but incomplete. Adding acupuncture, targeted physiotherapy, environmental modifications, and nutritional support can significantly improve outcomes and quality of life.
